UI band completion estimate: 88%

Top gaps:
1. Proving Grounds is intentionally gated by `debug=1` but behavior/intent is not documented in-app near entry.
2. Continue/save/download/copy controls are functionally guarded in handlers but not fully disabled or visually state-tied in some cases.
3. Large fixed/min heights and dense scroll containers may degrade usability on very short mobile viewports.
